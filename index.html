✦ The Full Story

The Eras of Year One

198,382 chat messages. One year. This is the story they tell.

May 15, 2025
Genesis
33,354 messages · 42 players
The server opens and the very first conversation is me and FluffiiBunnii going "lmao" and "hmmm." BiscuitBouncer shows up and immediately says "the laggg." tokyo comes in with "hai." Within 24 hours some absolute maniac named 0sca has already killed the Ender Dragon. We barely had iron tools. The very first "wb" is said. And yeah. That kinda set the tone for everything.
"he killed the dragon in like 15 mins lmao"— Cheddah01, May 16
June 2025
The Golden Age
53,415 messages · 40 players · busiest month ever
This month was genuinely unhinged. Arkansas Man shows up and immediately starts referring to himself in the third person. Nova and Yukimiracle have the first server wedding and BiscuitBouncer tries to bail halfway through. We added a marriage plugin and it caused absolute chaos. CaduCL28 walks in and says "I have returned" like an anime character. XDragon says "hello" for the first time and then just never logs off for the rest of the year.
"god do I love this server"— Nova2O09, June 5 (one month before they leave forever)
July 2025
The Quiet Summer
208 messages · 8 players
Summer hit and everyone disappeared lol. 208 messages. The entire month. 8 people. The server was basically in a coma but the builds were all still there just sitting in the dark waiting for someone to come back.
August – September 2025
The Comeback
25,572 messages · new players arriving
And people did come back. Powerofthedogs90 shows up and says "k" and then immediately starts ringing bells 2,585 times (we still dont know why). EaglesDJDan walks in asking "voicechat?" and proceeds to mine 1,766 diamonds. coshmee and Rodrigo find each other and start building the most beautiful town any of us have ever seen. VoidHeart joins as HeartOfChaos1290 calling everyone "cheese man" and nobody knows what they're about to become.
"remember when i crashed the server with lava"— BiscuitBouncer, August 13
October – November 2025
The Second Peak
58,639 messages · 43 players · 5.9M block actions in one day
This was the absolute peak of everything. 43 players talking at once. November 5th had 5.9 million block actions in a single day which is actually insane. Veystar joins and the first thing they do is propose to me (i said no). Eijan arrives screaming "YOO." Arkansas Man declares himself commander of the north legion army. The Justice for Cadu movement happens. VoidHeart goes full villain arc killing every mooshroom and endangered animal they can find. Halloween goes crazy. Everything is happening at once and it feels like the server will be like this forever.
"justice for Cadu!!!!"— Yugodz7, November 1
Late November – December 2025
The Departure
Activity falling · core players going silent one by one
One by one people just stopped showing up. FluffiiBunnii said "ill be back in a bit maybe" on October 26. They didnt come back. Arkansas Man's last words were "I am Arkansas man commander of the north legion army. Goodnight." GideonTheDog said "oop" and vanished. deadsilent said "imma get off for the night. gnite" and that was it. VoidHeart left with a bang on December 10. You dont really notice it happening until you look at the numbers and realize how quiet it got.
"god I love this server"— VoidHeart, November 13 (three days before going quiet)
January – March 2026
The Faithful Few
18,079 messages · the core group holds the line
Smaller but not gone. XDragon, BiscuitBouncer, coshmee, miningking11, and CaduCL28 just kept showing up. XDragon went on a completionist tear and killed every hostile mob type in the game. coshmee hit a 60,706 to 1 kill death ratio which honestly shouldnt be possible. Season 2 talk started. The world was quieter but the people still here really loved it and thats what mattered.
"cozy crafters season 2 confirmed??"— Yugodz7, March 14
April 2026
The Anniversary
One year · 198,382 messages · 6,656 hours played · 1.4M mobs killed
And here we are. One year of blocks placed and broken. Of "wb" and "ty." Of weddings and wars and Arkansas Man and pufferfish dinners and FluffiiBunnii jumping half a million times and VoidHeart breaking past the world border. I dug through every piece of data this server has ever created to put this page together and honestly it made me realize something. Every single number up there is a moment one of you chose to spend here on this little passion project ive created. And thats pretty cool, so thank you :)
🧡

Thank you for making this place what it is. Here's to Year Two.
